Who We Are

Built by
Engineers
for Professionals

Pandag was founded in 2021 with a simple conviction: that the commercial landscapes of the modern world — the corporate campuses, golf courses, solar farms, schools and public parks that shape how we live and work — deserve a better way to be cared for.

Labor was getting harder to find. Crews were getting older, and the next generation wasn't showing up. Injuries — the quiet, daily kind, not the ones that make the news — kept happening. Emissions rules were tightening. Clients wanted more, faster, cleaner. And the operators in the middle of all of it were asked, every season, to just figure it out.

We started Pandag because we didn't think anyone should have to just figure it out anymore.

Our Values

What Drives
Everything We Build

01
Precision Engineering
We obsess over tolerances, materials and field performance. Every component is selected to meet the demands of professional daily use — not just to pass a showroom test.
02
Operator Safety First
From two-hand interlocks on our splitters to AI obstacle detection on our mowers, safety is never an afterthought. It is designed into every product from day one.
03
Sustainable Innovation
Electric powertrains, low-noise motors and precision application technology reduce environmental impact. The land our customers work on deserves protection too.

Customer Partnership
We don't just ship products — we build long-term relationships. Our field service teams, training programmes and 48-hour response commitment all reflect that.
05
Continuous Improvement
Feedback from operators in 50+ countries feeds directly back into product development. Every generation of PANDAG equipment is measurably better than the last.
06
Global Reach, Local Support
An expanding network of certified dealers and service partners ensures that wherever you operate, expert support is never far away.
