Product Highlights

What Sets Each Machine Apart

RTK / 4G Navigation
Centimeter-level RTK navigation keeps each pass accurately aligned and eliminates any overlaps across large areas.
Automatic Path Planning
Smart algorithms automatically plan and optimize mowing routes for efficient coverage of large scale sites.
AI-Vision Obstacle
UltraSense™ AI vision detects people, animals and equipment in real time for safe autonomous operation.
Auto Recharge
Automatically returns to the charging dock, recharges and seamlessly resumes mowing from the exact previous location.
Multi-Zone Management
Easily store and switch between 100+ field maps, supporting multiple sites and operational zones with one machine.
App Fleet Service
Monitor, schedule and control multiple units in real time from a single, convenient mobile platform.
High-Speed Dual Blades
Counter-rotating hardened steel blades spin at high RPM to chip branches up to 100mm diameter into uniform mulch in seconds.
Auto Feed Control
Intelligent feed-roller speed adjusts automatically to material thickness — prevents jamming and maintains consistent throughput without operator adjustment.
Safety Auto-Stop
Reverse-feed emergency stop activates instantly if an obstruction or unsafe condition is detected — protecting the operator and the machine.
Low Noise Motor
Optimised blade geometry and vibration-damped housing reduce operating noise to levels suitable for residential neighbourhoods and noise-sensitive sites.
Tow-Behind Design
Standard tow hitch attaches to any farm vehicle or tractor for easy repositioning across large sites without manual carrying.
Large Capacity Collection
Wide-mouth discharge chute directs chips into a collection bag or trailer. Minimise ground mess and make clean-up part of the chipping workflow.
High-Force Hydraulic Cylinder
Precision hydraulic ram delivers powerful, consistent splitting force through even the most gnarled hardwood — no stalling, no partial splits.
Two-Hand Safety Interlock
Both control levers must be engaged simultaneously to activate the ram — a certified safety design that prevents single-hand or accidental operation.
Horizontal & Vertical Modes
Convert between horizontal and vertical splitting positions in seconds — horizontal for speed, vertical for heavy rounds too large to lift onto the beam.
Towable Frame
Road-legal tow hitch and fold-down log cradle mean the splitter travels with you — from woodlot to barn to storage without a trailer.
Fast Cycle Time
Rapid-return hydraulic valve minimises dead time between strokes — keeping throughput high for commercial firewood and timber operations.
Wide Log Capacity
Extended beam and wide wedge design accommodates logs up to 500mm in diameter — handles the big rounds other splitters struggle with.
Wide Rubber Tracks
Low ground-pressure rubber tracks spread load evenly — preventing soil compaction on sensitive agricultural land while maintaining grip on mud, gravel and steep hillsides.
Steep-Grade Climbing
Differential track drive and low centre of gravity allow the carrier to ascend and descend grades that wheeled vehicles cannot safely negotiate.
High-Capacity Flat Bed
Reinforced steel platform handles heavy bulk loads — from harvested produce and timber to construction materials and machinery parts.
Optional Remote Control
Wireless remote operation keeps the operator at a safe distance on unstable slopes, near hazardous material or in confined spaces where riding is dangerous.
Anti-Rollover Stability
Active stability monitoring and wide track stance prevent tipping during cornering and side-hill traversing — critical for safe hillside operation.
Electric Drive System
Zero-emission electric motors provide instant torque, low maintenance and quiet operation — suitable for use inside greenhouses, livestock buildings and noise-restricted areas.
Hydraulic Tipping Bed
Single-switch hydraulic ram raises the tipping bed to full angle in seconds — rapid unloading of soil, aggregate, grain and green waste with no manual handling.
Pure Electric Powertrain
High-capacity battery pack delivers full-day runtime on a single charge. Zero emissions — approved for indoor warehouses, covered markets and emission-controlled zones.
High-Payload Capacity
Reinforced chassis and dump box engineered for heavy commercial loads — move more per trip and reduce total journey time on site.
Smart Dashboard
Digital instrument cluster shows battery level, payload weight, tipping angle and fault alerts — giving the operator complete situational awareness at a glance.
Rollover Protection
Full ROPS-compliant cab frame and tipping-lock safety pin prevent accidental bed drop and protect the operator under any operating condition.
Compact Site Maneuverability
Short turning radius and compact dimensions allow the tipper to navigate tight construction corridors, barn aisles and urban delivery zones with ease.
